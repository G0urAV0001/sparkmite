ABOUT SPARK

MITE SPARK is a Student Prototyping and Research Knowledge Lab under the Department of Mechatronics. The lab promotes innovation, research, and hands-on learning by enabling students to design, prototype, and develop engineering solutions using modern tools.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Promote Innovation

Fostering practical problem solving and out-of-the-box thinking among students.

Prototyping Tools

Providing access to state-of-the-art tools and equipment for rapid prototyping.

Support Research

Aiding students in research, national-level competitions, and startup initiatives.

Collaboration

Encouraging interdisciplinary collaboration to build holistic engineering solutions.

Ecosystem

Building a robust innovation and entrepreneurship ecosystem from the ground up.

FACILITIES & EQUIPMENT

3D Printing Lab

High-precision FDM and resin printers for rapid physical modeling and parts creation.

Cutting & Fabrication

Laser cutters, CNC routers, and basic power tools for structural fabrication.

Soldering & Welding

Advanced soldering stations and spot welding machines for battery packs and joints.

PCB & Electronics

Testing equipment, oscilloscopes, and tools for embedded systems development.

Safety & Misc

Top-tier safety gear and miscellaneous equipment ensuring a secure lab environment.
